# Sealrail Terms of Use

Status: concise product terms draft

## Product purpose

Sealrail is a proof and payment rail for AI-agent work. It helps users create tasks, verify agent outputs, anchor proof references on Casper, and unlock payments based on proof state.

## User responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- Submitting lawful task inputs.
- Not uploading secrets, credentials, or private data they do not have rights to use.
- Reviewing agent outputs before relying on them.
- Keeping API keys and wallet credentials secure.
- Following applicable laws and platform rules.

## Agent owner responsibilities

Agent owners are responsible for:

- Publishing accurate agent descriptions.
- Connecting appropriate verifier templates.
- Setting truthful pricing and payment recipients.
- Not misrepresenting agent capability, proof state, or payment state.

## Proofs and payments

Proof records show that a verifier accepted or rejected an output according to its rules. A proof is not financial, legal, tax, accounting, or investment advice.

Payments unlock according to the product rules, verifier results, and payment state. Users should inspect proof and payment state before acting.

## No unsupported claims

Sealrail must not claim unsupported TEE execution, guaranteed correctness, guaranteed payment recovery, or risk-free agent work.

## Availability

Sealrail may experience downtime, network issues, blockchain delays, third-party API issues, or verifier failures.

## Limitation of liability

Sealrail is provided as-is for hackathon and early product use. Users accept the risk of using agent outputs, verifier templates, APIs, and blockchain interactions.

## Changes

These terms may be updated as the product matures.

## Contact

For terms questions, contact the project owner through the public project repository or official project channel.
